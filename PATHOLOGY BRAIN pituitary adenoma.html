Pituitary adenomas are tumours that arise in the pituitary gland.

Aetiology: Unknown.

Clinical presentation: hormonal imbalances, visual disturbances.

Microadenomas - less than 10mm

Macroadenomas -greater than 10mm

MRI appearance:
T1: Hypointense
T2: Unpredicatble variable signal
T1 contrast enhanced: Hyperintense
